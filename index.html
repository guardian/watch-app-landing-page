Introducing Moments from the Guardian App

Moments from the Guardian app delivers the very best of Guardian’s journalism straight to your Apple Watch. With a personal stream of news and stories you can access at a glance, you’ll get the whole picture, the whole time.

  • Takeover Moments

    Never miss out. Get the latest breaking news and goal alerts delivered straight to your wrist with rich notifications built for the Apple Watch

  • Just For You

    Get personally relevant content delivered throughout the day to your Apple Watch in a simple glanceable view. Moments are tailored for the time of day and day of week to always be relevant to you

  • Handoff

    Want to read the whole story? At any time you can use Apple’s Handoff feature to view an article or notification on your iPhone

  • Save For Later

    If you don't have the time to read now select ‘save for later’ and it will be waiting for you in the app to read at your own convenience

  • Know the Score

    Keep up to date on your favourite team with a simple glance. If you want to read the full match report simply Handoff to your iPhone
